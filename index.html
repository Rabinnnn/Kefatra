Did You Know?

Solar energy is one of the fastest-growing sources of renewable energy in the world? In 2020, solar power accounted for almost 4% of global electricity generation, up from just 0.1% in 2006. This exponential growth is due in part to the decreasing cost of solar panels, which has made solar energy more accessible and affordable to individuals and businesses alike.

Did You Know?

While it's true that solar panels are most effective in areas with lots of direct sunlight, they can still generate electricity even on cloudy or overcast days. In fact, Germany, which is not known for its sunny climate, is a world leader in solar energy production.
